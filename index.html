Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who hate losing important messages they accidentally dismissed.

🔔

Auto Capture

Works silently in the background. Every notification from every app is captured automatically — no setup needed per app.

🔍

Global Search

Search across your entire notification history instantly. Filter by app, date, read/unread, or full message text.

📊

Smart Grouping

Notifications grouped by app with unread counts. Tap any app to see every message with sender grouping inside chats.

Auto Clean

Set a retention period of 7, 15, 30 or 60 days. Old notifications are automatically deleted to keep storage clean.

Block Apps

Don't want to capture notifications from music or calling apps? Add them to the blocked list in one tap.
